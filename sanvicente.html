SAN VICENTE

San Vicente Beach, located in the municipality of San Vicente in Palawan, is home to the Long Beach, which spans an impressive 14 kilometers, making it the longest white sand beach in the Philippines. This unspoiled and tranquil destination offers stunning coastlines, crystal-clear waters, and a laid-back atmosphere, perfect for travelers seeking relaxation away from crowded tourist spots.

The area is also a gateway to nearby islands, snorkeling spots, and marine life exploration. San Vicente is gradually gaining attention as an eco-tourism hub, with plans for sustainable tourism to preserve its pristine environment.
